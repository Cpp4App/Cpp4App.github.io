Welcome to SeePrivacy!
Automated Contextual Privacy Policies Generation for Mobile Apps

Welcome to the home of SeePrivacy!

In a world where mobile applications are increasingly woven into our daily lives, understanding the privacy implications of using these applications has never been more critical. SeePrivacy is a revolutionary solution designed to address this challenge, making privacy policies more understandable and context-aware.

We propose a novel multi-modal framework, namely SeePrivacy, designed to automatically generate contextual privacy policies for mobile apps.

Our method synergistically combines CV-based mobile GUI understanding and NLP-based privacy policy document analysis, achieves an impressive performance.
